Ways to Pay

Various payment options are available for purchasing books and enrolling in programs.

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods to ensure a smooth and convenient checkout experience.

Cashapp $ParisMFrance

Send money directly to $ParisMFrance for book purchases and program enrollments. I do ask if you do use cashapp you send a message with the amount and the purpose of the payment. Thank you!

Venmo @Paris-M-France

Use Venmo to pay for books and programs by sending payments to @Paris-M-France. Please include a note with your payment indicating the purpose of the transaction.

Credit/Debit Cards

We accept all major credit and debit cards for online purchases. Simply enter your card details at checkout to complete your purchase securely.
